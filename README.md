Privacy Policy for Sukun
Last Updated: May 3, 2026

Your privacy is our priority. Sukun is designed to be a secure and private application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data Collection
Sukun does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Location Data: The app uses your location solely for core functionality (e.g., location-based features). This data is processed entirely on your device.

Personal Information: We do not ask for your name, email address, or any other identifying information.

2. Data Processing and Storage
All data generated within the app stays locally on your device. We do not have access to your location history or any other settings you configure within the app.

3. Third-Party Sharing
Since we do not collect any data, we do not share, sell, or rent your information to any third parties or advertising agencies.

4. Tracking and Cookies
Sukun does not use any tracking tools, analytics SDKs, or cookies.

5. Contact
If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you can contact us through our GitHub repository.
